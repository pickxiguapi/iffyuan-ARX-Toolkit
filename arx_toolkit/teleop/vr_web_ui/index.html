Instructions

Setup: Open https://loading... in Quest 3 browser, then tap "Start Controller Tracking".
Grip Button (side): Hold to activate arm control. The robot arm tracks your hand movement as a delta from the grip-press position.
Trigger Button (index finger): Press to open gripper; release to close.
Wrist Rotation: Roll and pitch of the controller map to the robot wrist.
